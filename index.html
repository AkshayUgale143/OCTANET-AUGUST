DESTINATION ,in Nashik

Nashik is an ancient holy city in Maharashtra, a state in western India. It’s known for its links to the “Ramayana” epic poem. On the Godavari River is Panchavati, a temple complex. Nearby, Lord Rama was thought to have bathed at Ram Kund water tank, today attended by Hindu devotees. Shri Kalaram Sansthan Mandir is an ancient shrine to Rama, while Rama and Sita are said to have worshipped at Sita Gufaa caves.And so on beautiful places in Nashik.


Umbrella Waterfalls

This is an amazing waterfall worth visiting near Nashik during the monsoon by all ages of people. This is a local picnic spot as it has hydro attractions like Wilson dam, hydro project and to see this umbrella-shaped waterfall. You can find more visitors during the weekends. People camp near the waterfall side and explore the waterfall surrounding the reservoir and its greenery to the fullest. Distance From Nashik: 68 Kms Entry Fee: Nil Timing: 06:00 AM to 06:00 PM Tips: You must carry an additional dress if you wish to swim. It is advisable to take your photo camera, camping kit, drinking water, food and a binocular.

Vihigaon Falls

This is a picturesque waterfall near Nashik, which drops from a 120-ft height from the Sahyadri Mountain Ranges. This waterfall looks amazing even from a distance. The unique thing about this waterfall is that 200 people can swim at a time in its cascade during the monsoon season. This is a high drop Plunge waterfall. You must be careful during the rainy season and avoid swimming and taking a bath under this waterfall. Yet, the nature-lovers will have a good time hiking this rainforest to reach the base of this waterfall. Distance From Nashik: 58 Kms Entry Fee: NIL Timing: 09:00 AM to 05:00 PM. You must check with the Maharashtra Tourism department during the monsoon season.

Durgawadi waterfall

Even before you reach Durgawadi you are sure to be met by the paralyzing beauty of the Trimbakeshwar mountains near Trimbak Road. The short journey of only 30 km from Nashik will provide you with an instant air of relief when you find yourself in the presence of the scintillating view of this waterfall.The falls is cascading down from a height of about 135 feet into a pool.The waterfall is idel spot for rappelling.
